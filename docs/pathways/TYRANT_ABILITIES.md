### Energy Transformation
**Sequence Requirement:** 1
**Spirituality Cost:** 16 per tick (toggle)
*(Cannot be copied, replicated, or used in artifacts)*

- Toggles a transformation state that grants **sustained flight** at speed 0.75.
- Deactivates automatically if flight is disabled externally.

### Calamity Creation
**Sequence Requirement:** 2
**Spirituality Cost:** 1200
**Cooldown:** 25 seconds
*(Cannot be copied)*

Four selectable modes:

**Mode 0 — Volcano**
- **Range:** 60 blocks
- **Hits:** **1 hit** per cast (spawns a volcano entity)
- **Damage:** **~41 damage per hit**.

**Mode 1 — Snow Storm**
- **Radius:** 60 blocks
- **Duration:** 30 seconds
- **Hits:** **150 hits** — one every 4 ticks (~49.4 base DPS)
- **Damage:** **~7.1 damage per hit**.
- Applies **Weakness (Level 1)** and **Slowness (Level 7)** for 5 seconds per hit.
- Creates SNOW and SNOW_BLOCK if griefing is enabled.

**Mode 2 — Drought**
- **Radius:** 90 blocks
- **Duration:** 30 seconds
- **Hits:** **150 hits** — one every 4 ticks (~49.4 base DPS)
- **Damage:** **~7.1 damage per hit**.
- Applies **Weakness (Level 1)** and **Slowness (Level 4)** for 5 seconds per hit.
- Creates FIRE, SAND, and SANDSTONE blocks if griefing is enabled.

**Mode 3 — Tornados**
- **Range:** 12 blocks
- Spawns **1 main tornado** + **30 additional tornadoes**.
- **Main tornado damage:** **~48.7 damage**.
- **Additional tornado damage:** 17 each (fixed, unscaled).

### Water Mastery
**Sequence Requirement:** 4
**Spirituality Cost:** 300
**Cooldown:** 5 seconds
*(Cannot be copied)*

Two selectable modes:

**Mode 0 — Water Wall**
- **Range:** 12 blocks; wall is **30 blocks wide** and **20 blocks tall**
- **Duration:** 30 seconds
- **Hit Interval:** Every 7 ticks
- **Hits:** Up to ~128 hits over full duration (per entity passing through)
- **Damage:** **~16.6 damage per hit**.
- **Knockback:** 1.4× on each hit.
- **Freezing Interaction:** Creates ICE blocks along the wall if a freezing interaction is active.

**Mode 1 — Flooding**
- **Radius:** 80 blocks
- **Duration:** 7 seconds (140 ticks)
- **Hit Interval:** Every 10 ticks
- Creates expanding circles of WATER blocks. Requires griefing to be enabled.

### Siren Song
**Sequence Requirement:** 5
**Spirituality Cost:** 180
**Cooldown:** 45 seconds
*(Cannot be copied or replicated)*

Three selectable modes:

**Mode 0 — Death Melody**
- **Radius:** 25 blocks
- **Duration:** 30 seconds

- **Damage:** **~14 damage per hit**.

**Mode 1 — Strengthening Melody**
- **Duration:** 30 seconds
- Grants allies within range **Strength** and **Speed**; applies a **1.5× damage multiplier** buff for the duration.

**Mode 2 — Dazing Song**
- **Radius:** 25 blocks
- **Duration:** 30 seconds
- **Effect Interval:** Every 18 ticks
- Applies to enemies:
  - **Slowness:** Level 3, 1 second
  - **Weakness:** Level 3, 1 second
  - **Blindness:** Level 3, 1 second

### Wind Manipulation
**Sequence Requirement:** 6
**Spirituality Cost:** 30
**Cooldown:** 1.5 seconds

Four selectable modes:

**Mode 0 — Wind Blade**
- **Range:** 50 blocks
- **Hits:** **1 hit** on contact
- **Damage:** **~13.4 damage per hit**.

**Mode 1 — Binding**
- **Radius:** 2.5 blocks
- **Duration:** 13 seconds
- **Effect Interval:** Every tick
- Pins entities in place. Countered by blink_escape interactions.

**Mode 2 — Flight**
- **Duration:** Up to 12 minutes (drains 3 spirituality per tick)
- Grants sustained flight.

**Mode 3 — Boost**
- Launches the caster upward if within 3.5 blocks of the ground.
